Isn't anime awesome?
I'm just here to convince you to watch anime. Anime is love, anime is life. I don't know where I would be without it. There's so many genres to explore, just like in western media. In my humble opinion, I suggest watching subbed anime over dubbed as I believe subbed catches and conveys human emotion so much better than dubbed can.
My Favorite Anime Characters
This first character is Killua Zoldyck from an anime called Hunter x Hunter. Hunter x Hunter is one of my favorite anime of all time, if not my favorite, and Killua is probably one of my favorite characters from the show.
This second character is Zoro from an anime called One Piece. One Piece is the longest running anime to my knowledge, with over 1000 episodes and it has been airing for over 20 years! And from day one, Zoro has been that guy.
This third character is Gojo Satoru from an anime called Jujutsu Kaisen. He is probably one of my favorite overall anime characters of all time, due to how unhinged he can be, as well as his personality and character design.
This fourth character is Tengen Uzui from an anime called Demon Slayer. Along with JJK, Demon Slayer is one of the more popular modern anime and Tengen is probably my favorite character from this one due to his personality.
